MORE LIVE ENTERTAINMENT

Located on the second floor expansion area of Rivers Casino, The Event Center serves as an intimate venue for Premier Entertainment.

Watch Grammy Award-winning artists, bands, and entertainers take center stage, offering an up-close-and-personal experience for all of their audience members.

No matter where you sit, every seat in the house will make you feel like you’re in the front row. Every performance will leave you wanting more, making it a go-to destination for music enthusiasts, casual listeners, and people looking for a night with their favorite performers.  

The Event Center has played host to a number of world-class performers like Michael Bolton, Cheap Trick, Melissa Etheridge, Jeffrey Osborne, and many others!
